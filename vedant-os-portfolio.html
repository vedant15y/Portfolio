ABOUT THE ARCHIVE

Manifest / Archive Logic

This portfolio is designed as a digital archive rather than a traditional personal website.

It documents creative work, technical projects, experiments, academic pursuits, and ideas developed over time.

The visual identity draws inspiration from terminal interfaces, cyberpunk operating systems, retro computing, and digital preservation aesthetics.

Rather than presenting information in a conventional format, the archive is intended to feel exploratory - as if navigating a personal operating system containing projects, concepts, and ongoing experiments.

This site is intentionally built to evolve over time. New projects, experiments, and discoveries are continuously added.

Think of it less as a portfolio and more as an interactive record of learning, making, designing, and building.
